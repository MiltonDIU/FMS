# RBAC Matrix & Workflow Diagrams – Faculty Management System (Final)

## Faculty Management System (FMS)

---

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** structure and **core system workflows** for the Faculty Management System (FMS). It establishes authority boundaries, approval responsibilities, data visibility rules, and operational flows required for governance, security, and accountability.

This document is part of **Milestone-1: Planning & Design** and does not include implementation or UI-level details.

---

## 2. User Role Definitions

### 2.1 Super Admin

* Full system-level authority
* Configures approval routing and role policies
* Has override and rollback permissions
* Can view all teacher publications and public faculty profiles

### 2.2 Admin

* Manages users and role assignments
* Creates and maintains initial faculty profiles
* Performs optional review of profile updates
* Controls publishing of public profiles
* Faculty & Department Wise statistics Dashboard
* Can view all teacher publications and public faculty profiles

### 2.3 Registrar Office

* Final approval authority for faculty profiles
* Ensures academic and institutional data validity
* Controls publishing of public profiles
* Faculty & Department Wise statistics Dashboard
* Individual teachers statistics/profile report
* Can view all teacher publications and public faculty profiles

### 2.4 Teacher

* Maintains own faculty profile
* Submits profile updates for approval
* Views personal dashboard and statistics
* Can view own publications and public faculty profiles

### 2.5 Research Team (Future Phase)

* Manages research and publication entries
* Faculty & Department Wise statistics Dashboard
* Individual teachers statistics/profile report
* Publication count per teacher
* Verifies author identity and affiliations
* Public profile access for all faculty members
* Can view all teacher publications and public faculty profiles

### 2.6 IT / Developer

* Technical maintenance and system support
* No access to academic or approval data

> A single user may hold **multiple roles simultaneously**.

## 4. Data Visibility Scope Rules

* **Teacher:** Own profile, publications, and personal statistics only
* **Department Head:** Statistics limited to own department
* **Faculty Dean:** Statistics for all departments under the faculty
* **Admin / Registrar Office:** System-wide visibility including all teacher publications and public faculty profiles
* **Research Team:** Department/faculty wise research statistics, individual teacher publications, and public profile access
* **Super Admin:** Unrestricted access, including all teacher publications and public faculty profiles

---

## 5. Profile Update & Approval Workflow

### 5.1 Standard Profile Update Flow

```
Teacher (Draft)
      ↓
Optional Admin Review
      ↓
Registrar Office Approval
      ↓
Published Profile (Live)
```

* Teachers, Admin, or Registrar Office can update or add all teacher-related information, including personal info, education, publications, experience, membership, awards, and other relevant academic data.
* All edits are saved as draft versions
* Only one approved version can be live at any time
* Rejected versions remain archived

---

## 6. Version Control & Audit Workflow

* Each submission creates a new profile version
* Approved versions are timestamped and archived
* Admin or Registrar Office may restore previous approved versions
* All actions are logged with user, role, and time

---

## 7. Administrative Role Assignment Workflow

```
Admin Assigns Role
      ↓
System Auto-Creates Experience (Start Date)
      ↓
Role Removal / Expiry
      ↓
Experience End Date Auto-Updated
```

* Academic designation remains unchanged
* Only one administrative role allowed per department at a time

---

## 8. Experience Management Rules

* Experiences may be system-generated or manually entered
* Teachers and authorized Admin/Registrar users may edit experience records
* All experience edits follow the same approval workflow

---

## 9. Notification & Escalation Rules

* Profile submission notifications to reviewers
* Pending approval reminders
* Rejection feedback to teachers
* Escalation to Super Admin if approval stalls

---

## 10. Exception & Override Rules

* Super Admin may override approval flow
* Emergency rollback supported
* Approval authority routing is configurable
